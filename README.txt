Author: Phan Hoang An Nguyen

Description: Data cleaning, EDA and building logistic regression to build predictive model for readmission probability of diabetic patients. Most importantly, applied Shapley Recursive Feature Elimination with Cross-validation (ShapRFECV) and compared model's accuracy with different study.

Dataset name: Diabetes 130-US Hospitals for Years 1999-2008

Dataset source: https://archive.ics.uci.edu/dataset/296/diabetes+130-us+hospitals+for+years+1999-2008

Published article used for comparison: Kanu, D. K. S., Kathmandu, N., & Khanal, M. Implementation of Big Data Analytics on Diabetes 130-US Hospitals for year 1999-2008 for predicting patient readmission.
